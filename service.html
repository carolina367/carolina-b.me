TAMU Global Ambassador

A Global Ambassador with Halliburton Engineering Global Programs is a leadership role for engineering students who have had an international experience. The role involves representing the Global Programs through peer mentorship and outreach, building relationships with the College of Engineering, sharing experiences, and promoting global-mindedness among students. Ambassadors also have the opportunity to develop professional skills such as marketing, recruitment, leadership, public speaking, peer leadership, and self-evaluation through various outreach events and experiences.

MentorSHPE

MentorSHPE is a program that facilitates one-to-one mentoring relationships that support personal and professional growth and development. It allows participants to gain new knowledge and insights, and build new skills for both mentors and mentees.

SHPE Junior

SHPE Junior The Jones Elementary Science Club is a collaborative initiative between TAMU SHPE and Anson Jones Elementary School. The aim is to broaden the reach of STEM education by engaging K-5 students in fun, hands-on activities. The students, who are enthusiastic participants in the club, get to experience the wonders of STEM firsthand. The club meets bi-weekly, serving as a platform for TAMU SHPE to contribute to their community and inspire young minds about the potential of STEM.

Code2College Alumni Coordinator

As a Code2College Alumni Coordinator, conducted 5 career-advancing workshops, benefiting 10 C2C alumni attending A&M. Also secured $3,000 worth of corporate tech donations, positively impacting the education of 25 underprivileged students.

TAMU Build

TAMU Build is a student-led organization that converts 40-foot shipping containers into fully-functional and portable Texas Aggie Medical Clinics (TAMCs), which are shipped domestically and internationally to serve communities in need. The overarching goal of TAMU Build is to design, construct, and deploy these clinics worldwide as a tribute to the lives lost in the collapse of the Aggie Bonfire.
